# Product Case Study — Scan & Safe

## 1. Problem

Many consumers struggle to understand skincare ingredient lists. Products often contain ingredients that may irritate sensitive skin, clog pores, or worsen acne, but ingredient labels use complex chemical terminology that is difficult for users to interpret.

As a result, consumers frequently purchase skincare products that are not suitable for their skin type.


## 2. Target Users

Primary users include:

* Individuals with acne-prone skin
* Consumers with sensitive skin
* Skincare beginners who want ingredient transparency
* Health-conscious consumers evaluating cosmetic products

Example Persona:

**Name:** Aisha
**Age:** 21
**Skin Type:** Oily / Acne-prone

Pain Points:

* Cannot interpret ingredient labels
* Purchases trending products without understanding ingredients
* Wants a quick way to check product safety


## 3. Product Vision

Create a tool that helps users quickly determine whether a skincare product is safe for their skin type by analyzing ingredient lists and highlighting potential risks.

The goal is to make skincare ingredient transparency accessible and easy to understand.


## 4. Key Features

* Ingredient safety analysis
* Acne-trigger detection
* Skin-type personalization
* Product safety score generation


## 5. Product Workflow

User enters ingredients
↓
System identifies known ingredients
↓
Risk scoring algorithm evaluates safety
↓
Safety score and warnings are generated


## 6. Competitive Analysis

Existing tools such as skincare ingredient scanners provide general safety ratings, but often lack personalization based on specific skin concerns such as acne triggers.

Scan & Safe focuses on personalized ingredient risk analysis based on skin type.


## 7. Future Product Roadmap

Potential future features include:

* Barcode scanning for skincare products
* Ingredient detection using image recognition (OCR)
* Product search database
* Mobile application version
* Expanded ingredient dataset
