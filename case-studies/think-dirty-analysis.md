Think Dirty Product Analysis
1. Product Overview

Think Dirty is a mobile application that helps users understand the safety of cosmetic and skincare products by analyzing their ingredients.

Users can scan product barcodes or search for products in the database to receive ingredient safety ratings and potential health impact information.

The goal of the product is to increase transparency in the cosmetics industry and help consumers make safer purchasing decisions.

2. Problem the Product Solves

Consumers often struggle to understand cosmetic ingredient labels.

Problems include:

Ingredient lists contain complex chemical names

Consumers cannot easily identify harmful or irritating ingredients

Marketing claims often mislead users about product safety

As a result, many users purchase products without understanding their potential health or skin impact.

Think Dirty simplifies this process by translating ingredient lists into easy-to-understand safety scores.

3. Key Features

Core features of Think Dirty include:

Barcode scanning for cosmetic products

Ingredient safety ratings

Product database with ingredient information

Product comparison tools

Educational content about cosmetic ingredients

These features help users quickly evaluate products before purchasing.

4. Product Strengths

 Simple User Experience- Scanning a product barcode provides instant results.

 Large Ingredient Database-The app contains a large dataset of cosmetic ingredients.
 
 Consumer Education-Think Dirty helps users learn about ingredients and their potential effects.
 
 Transparency Focus-The app promotes transparency in the cosmetics industry.

5. Product Weaknesses

   
Limited Personalization-Ingredient risk is not personalized for specific skin types or conditions such as acne or sensitivity.

Lack of Contextual Risk-Some ingredients are labeled risky even when they are safe in low concentrations.

Limited Skincare Guidance-The app provides safety scores but does not guide users toward better alternatives or personalized skincare routines.

6. Opportunities for Improvement

There are several opportunities to improve the product:

Personalized ingredient risk analysis based on skin type

Ingredient explanations tailored for different skin concerns

Skincare recommendations based on user needs

Better transparency around ingredient scoring methodology

7. Product Lessons for Scan & Safe

The analysis of Think Dirty helped shape the design of Scan & Safe.

Key insights include:

Ingredient safety tools should be easy to understand

Users benefit from clear safety scores

Personalization based on skin type and skin concerns can improve the usefulness of ingredient analysis tools

Scan & Safe builds on these insights by focusing on ingredient risk detection tailored to specific skin conditions such as acne-prone or sensitive skin.
